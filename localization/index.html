[ 04 / ENTERPRISE PAIN POINTS ]

The Challenges of Indic Language Localisation

Many enterprise localisation programs are designed around Latin-script languages and encounter significant barriers when deployed across Indic markets at scale.

01 // Linguistic

Linguistic Complexity

  • Multiple writing systems and scripts
  • Regional language variations and dialects
  • Industry-specific terminology requirements
  • Cultural adaptation and contextual accuracy needs
02 // Design

Design & Formatting Challenges

  • Text expansion breaking original layouts
  • Layout inconsistencies across locales
  • Font rendering problems for complex scripts
  • Multilingual publishing complexities
03 // Operational

Operational Challenges

  • Multiple vendor dependencies and fragmentation
  • Inconsistent quality standards across vendors
  • Slow turnaround times under volume pressure
  • Difficult cross-team project coordination
04 // Scale

Scale Challenges

  • Managing multiple Indian languages simultaneously
  • Maintaining terminology consistency at scale
  • Ensuring brand alignment across regions
  • Supporting continuous content updates
