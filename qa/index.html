[ 02 / WHY INDEPENDENT QA ]

The Value of Independent Review

Internal teams cannot objectively review their own work. Independent QA provides the unbiased verification that ensures publication accuracy before it reaches your audience.

Unbiased Review

Truly Independent Perspective

SuperDTP's QA specialists are independent of your production team — providing a fresh, unbiased review that identifies issues internal reviewers may overlook due to familiarity with the content.

Flexible Scope

Stand-alone or Integrated

IQA can be integrated into your production cycle as a final quality gate, or commissioned as a stand-alone assignment for third-party review of any completed publication.

Actionable Output

Reports & Corrections

Every IQA assignment includes a detailed report documenting all findings and modifications. Optionally, SuperDTP can implement corrections during the review process and return updated files.
