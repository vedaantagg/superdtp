Make Your PDFs Accessible, Compliant, and User-Friendly

PDFs are widely used for corporate reports, manuals, forms, policy documents, training materials, brochures, and public-facing communication. However, not every PDF is accessible to users who rely on screen readers or assistive technologies.

SuperDTP provides specialized PDF remediation services to make documents accessible, structured, navigable, and compliant with global accessibility standards such as WCAG, PDF/UA, Section 508, ADA, and AODA. Our PDF accessibility experts improve the internal structure of documents so they can be interpreted correctly by assistive technologies while preserving visual design and brand quality.

What We Remediate

  • Reading Order
  • Heading Structure
  • Tags and Document Structure
  • Tables and Lists
  • Hyperlinks and Bookmarks
  • Fillable Forms
  • Alternative Text for Images
  • Language Attributes
  • Document Metadata
  • Color Contrast Review
  • Screen Reader Compatibility
  • Accessibility Validation
